## 香港生育意愿调查

香港生活是 costly 的，这也是人们不想要孩子的原因。调查了2300人，70%的受访者说他们不想要孩子。

From mid-January to mid-February this year, the group surveyed nearly 2,300 people. More than 70 percent of respondents said they do not want to have children. 28 percent indicated inclination to have a child, marking an increase of about two percentage points year on year.

## 年龄差异与推迟生育趋势

The age group between 19 and 29 years old is the most unwilling to have a child. The 30-39-year-old age group meanwhile is the most open to giving birth. The Women's Association said that reflects the trend of Hong Kong women preferring to delay motherhood.

## 政策建议

The group recommends more family-friendly policies, including tax relief and increasing child care services.
