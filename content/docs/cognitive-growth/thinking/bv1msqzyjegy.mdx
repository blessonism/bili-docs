## 赌徒心理与沉没成本

B：The fallacy of sunk cost. It's what gamblers do. They throw good money after bad, thinking they can turn their luck around. It's like, "I've already spent this much money, or time or whatever, I gotta keep going." No, there's no reward at the end of this game.
