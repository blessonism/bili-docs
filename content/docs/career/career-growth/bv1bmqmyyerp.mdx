## 被拒绝是一件很好的事

当你 interview for a job，你会知道10%的公司，但你不知道90%的公司。你会被拒绝，然后你会回家。为什么？因为你在一个地方不成功，你被拒绝了。但被拒绝，那是一件很好的事。

## 融入团队的重要性

When I'm hiring, I always look for people who can blend. Would they blend into the group I got? Would the group come to me later and say, "Alright, why did you do this to us?" Or would they come to me and say, "He is so wonderful"?

## 在团队中勇于承担责任

When you're part of a team or leader of a team and something's gone wrong, 你可以说："I have screwed up." Now everybody knows it ain't you, but 也许因为 you are just taking the responsibility，这是一件很重要的事情。
